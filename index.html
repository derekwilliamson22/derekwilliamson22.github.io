myFlock

Chicken Tracking and Customer Service App

Keeping chickens is a very rewarding experience. Since they’re relatively low maintenance, it can be easy to lose track of their needs. myFlock is a mobile-first tool that helps a user track each chicken’s quality of life. The user will be able to record certain data points that enable them to better understand the life of each chicken. Based on those needs, the user may request or purchase services provided by Northern Flock, a backyard-chicken resource.

  • Duration: Two Week Sprint
  • Technologies Used: Javascript, React, Node, SQL, CSS and Material UI

Lake Elmo Aero

Customer Service Request App

Lake Elmo Aero Project is a customer-service based app. It will enable pilots and aviation enthusiasts to conveniently request fuel services and other aircraft maintenance for their aircraft provided by the Lake Elmo Aero FBO.

Upon receiving a service request, Lake Elmo Aero FBO will receive notification via email and SMS. Service requests can be accessed by logging into the “Admin Portal”. Upon completion of the service request, Lake Elmo Aero FBO will have the ability to notify the customer via email that their request has been completed.

  • Duration: Two Week Sprint
  • Group Members Include:
    • Leah Brenny
    • Justus Witmer
    • Tyler Jorenby
  • Technologies Used: Javascript, React, Node, SQL, CSS and a variety of third party dependencies.
